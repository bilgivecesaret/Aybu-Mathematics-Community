Featured Mathematicians

Euclid

Known as the "Father of Geometry", Euclid authored the Elements, one of the most influential works in mathematics history.

Pythagoras

Famous for the Pythagorean theorem, he made fundamental contributions to mathematics and philosophy.

Archimedes

Known for his principle of buoyancy and work on levers, he was one of the greatest mathematicians of antiquity.

Isaac Newton

Co-inventor of calculus and discoverer of the laws of motion and universal gravitation.

Leonhard Euler

Prolific mathematician who made important discoveries in calculus, graph theory, and number theory.
